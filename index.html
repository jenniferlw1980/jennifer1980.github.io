Courage is the abilty to proceed with your life knowing that the odds are stacked against you. It's the ability to stand straight and walk tall. Courage requires grit and passion.

Our Mission Statement

We all have journeys in life, and some are harder than others. The truth is life can be downright brutal and grueling. It can bring the strongest to thier knees and turn the bravest into fragile humans. Unfortunately, not everyone is able to find the strength to withstand the whispers, overcome the stares, and confront the pejorative comments. Some will fail. Unbreakable is a non-profit community site dedicated to supporting those affected by adversity. We offer a safe and compassionate space to share stories and seek support. It is our mission to help every person take back thier life on thier terms.
